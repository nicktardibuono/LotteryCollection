Lottery Collection

  • High Concept: Collect lottery tickets and get the highest score in 60 seconds.
  • Player Experience Goals: Have fun, avoiding enemies and collecting lottery tickets.
  • Genre: Platformer/Arcade/Race
  • Game play: The player moves left and right and jumps using the arrow keys.
  • Prototype goal: Parse inting numbers and camera follows.
  • Features: Random scoring system and countdown.
  • Setting/Premise: Random street.
  • Story: Your trying to collect the most lottery tickets in 60 seconds while avoiding the leperchauns.
  • Target Audience: Casual Gamers
  • Play time: One minute.
  • Strategies: Moving as fast as possible and to get the highest score.
